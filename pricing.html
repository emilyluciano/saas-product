General Questions

Would I need to enter my credit card info to sign up?
No. You can sign up and use 5 & Dime for 14 days without entering your credit card info. At the end of your trial, you can then decide what plan suits you best.
Can I cancel my account at any time I want?
Yes. If you ever decide 5 & Dime is not working for you, then cancel whenever you like.
How long are your contracts?
All plans are monthly unless you sign up for an annual or bi-weekly plan.
Do you offer any discounted plans?
Yes, we offer a 15% discount on an annual and a 30% discount on a biennial plans, when they are paid upfront.
Can I change my plan later on?
Absolutely! You can upgrade whenever you like or downgrade your plan at any time.
Is 5 & Dime PCI Compliant or PCI Certified?
Yes. We are a certified Level 1 PCI DSS compliant. This means all your data and customer information is ultra secure.
